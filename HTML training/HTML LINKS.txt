HTML LINKS - HYPERLINKS

HTML LINKS are hyperlinks(jumps to another link when you click on it)
#Doesn't have to be text.Can be an image or HTML elment

Syntax: The HTML <a> tag defines a hyperlink

eg<a href="url">link text</a>

The most important attribute of the <a> element is the href attribute, which indicates the link's destination.The link text is the part that will be visible to the reader.

eg<a href="https://www.w3schools.com/">Visit W3Schools.com!</a>

By default in Browers they show up as:
An unvisited link is underlined and blue
A visited link is underlined and purple
An active link is underlined and red

#Can be styled with CSS for another look
